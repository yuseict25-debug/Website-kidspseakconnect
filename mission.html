Our Mission

Fun & Practical English for Kids!

Moving from "Study" to "Experience"

KidSpeak Connect is run by students who have extensive experience living abroad. As native-level speakers, we noticed that many English learners in Japan focus heavily on grammar and testing ("studying"), but rarely get the chance to use English in a fun, natural way ("experiencing"). We host workshops mainly in Kawasaki and we are based in Tokyo!

We provide a space where you can use the English you've learned to communicate, play, and connect with others.

"English is a tool for connection."

We want you to feel the joy of understanding and being understood.
